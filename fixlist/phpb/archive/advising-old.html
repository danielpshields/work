Advising

Students will work closely with a pre-health advisor throughout the program. You will be required to meet with your advisor at least once per semester to discuss your current courses and plan for the next semester. Advisors are also available Monday-Friday if you ever have questions that need to be answered. During your first and last semesters you will take a pre-health seminar course with the pre-health advisors to gain more in-depth knowledge about preparing for the profession and the application process.

Walk-in advising is available in Farrior Hall. Just sign in at the front desk when you arrive.
