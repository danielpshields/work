The University of Florida's Pre-health Post-Baccalaureate Program (PHPB) is a "career-changer" program offering dedicated and motivated students an opportunity to complete academic pre-requisite courses for admission to medical school, dental school, and other health professional graduate programs. Students will complete the majority of courses in a small classroom setting, experiencing the outstanding teaching faculty at the University of Florida. This program assists students in developing academic skills, application skills, and professional competencies necessary for the application to professional healthcare schools.

Mission

The University of Florida's Pre-health Post-Baccalaureate Program (PHPB) in the Office of Health and Legal Professions Advising (OHLPA), seeks to empower and support college graduates who are interested in pursuing a healthcare profession but have taken few or no science courses required for admission to medical, dental, or other healthcare related graduate programs.
