Someone Special

It’s nice to share the forest with someone. There are things out there that can’t be described by images or words alone. And even to walk hand-in-hand, side-by-side with someone, there are things the other will still never see.

Two Friends

Away from the cars and keys, just you, you, my camera and me, we walk through the forest trees and tall grass and I search the ground for any sign of someone from before. It’s mostly just balloons out here. They fell from so far away to rest in a place so seldom seen by others.

Deep Trees

I grabbed this tree or this tree grabbed me. I shot it with my mamiya. And it turned out dark. It was originally dark in reality. And that makes me sad to think about.

The End of the Road

I grew tired of the endless fight and so I rest my eyes and soul and mind.
